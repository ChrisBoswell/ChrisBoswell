Welcome to Flux Labs!

Design

Our associates will work with you to create your vision. Whether this is your company logo, school emblem, or a gift for a friend, we work until we have a 3D model that satisfies you!

Print

Your model is printed on our high quality Prusa i3 MK3S+ printers. We also us the MMU 2.0 upgrade, allowing us to print in up to 5 colors! We use PLA printing material with high density infill to ensure a long-lasting product.

Deliver

Have your custom designed model shipped to you fast with several delivery options! Lead time around two weeks for each unique design.

We look forward to helping you with all of your modeling and printing needs!
