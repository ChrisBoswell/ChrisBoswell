Flux Labs is an Austin-based company that loves 3D printing. We want to help companies increase their brand awareness by helping them produce high quality custom 3D prints of their logo and brand related merchandise. We value input from the customer at all stages of the production process so that we can provide the most satisfying representation of the brands we work with.
